🎯 THE PROBLEM SOLVED:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  Original Issue:
  ───────────────
  "Boundary also red and white block so some time it is say boundary is car 
   and it is select alternately"

  Root Cause:
  ───────────
  Road markers (red/white blocks) and RC car both red → color-based detection
  confused them, causing alternating selection

  Solution:
  ────────
  Switch from color-based (HSV) to intensity-based (adaptive threshold)
  detection, combined with intelligent spatial filtering


💡 HOW IT WORKS:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  Detection Pipeline:
  ──────────────────
  
  Camera Frame → Grayscale → Blur → Adaptive Threshold → Morphology
                                                              ↓
                                              Find & Filter Contours
                                                              ↓
                                  ┌──────────────┬───────────┴───────────┐
                                  ↓              ↓                       ↓
                            Outer Boundary  Inner Boundary        Other Objects
                                                              (potential car)
                                  ↓              ↓                       ↓
                            Create Road Mask (bitwise_xor)       Apply 5 Filters:
                                  ↓                            1. Location OK?
                                  ↓                            2. Size OK?
                                  ↓                            3. Shape OK?
                                  ↓                            4. Movement OK?
                                  ↓                            5. In road? OK?
                                  ↓                                     ↓
                           Draw Visualizations ←─────────────── Select Car
                                  ↓
                              Display Output


  Why Adaptive Threshold:
  ──────────────────────
  OLD: Color-based detection
      • Detects "red" pixels
      • Road markers are red → detected as potential car
      • Confusion and alternation

  NEW: Intensity-based detection
      • Detects brightness contrast
      • Works on any color markers (red, white, blue, etc.)
      • Robust to lighting changes
      • Proven to work on your circular track


  Intelligent Car Filtering:
  ────────────────────────
  Even if markers are detected, they're filtered out by:
  
  ✗ Wrong location    (markers at edges, car in center)
  ✗ Wrong size        (markers huge or tiny, car moderate)
  ✗ Wrong shape       (markers elongated, car square-ish)
  ✗ Static position   (markers never move, car moves)
  ✗ Outside road      (markers on edges, car on road)
  
  Only contours passing ALL tests → Identified as car

❓ TROUBLESHOOTING:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  Camera Won't Open?
  • Check permissions: Settings → Security & Privacy → Camera
  • Try different camera index: --camera 1

  Boundaries Not Detected?
  • Ensure good lighting on track
  • Verify track has clear boundaries
  • Test with extract_clean_boundaries.py first

  Car Not Detected?
  • Ensure car is in lower 60% of frame
  • Car must be within 60% of road width
  • System will fall back to red detection if needed

  False Detections?
  • Adjust spatial constraints in code
  • Fine-tune size thresholds
  • Reduce temporal distance for stricter movement
